Recipe for Falooda

Description

A beloved summer treat, Faloodas are cool and refreshing.

Quench your desire for sweet with this rich and creamy dessert!

Ingredients

  • Vermicelli
  • Syrup
  • Milk
  • Ice cream

Steps

  1. Heat syrup
  2. Make jello
  3. Cook vermicelli
  4. Layer into tall galsses
